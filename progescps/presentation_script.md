## Welcome to Programmed Escapist!

**Programmed Escapist** is an educational turn-based RPG where you play as a programmer-hero battling software threats and malware in a digital world. Choose from 6 unique character classes, explore different locations, complete quests, and build your reputation with various factions.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Character Selection
When you start the game, you'll choose from 6 different character classes:

- **Debugger**: Tank class with high HP and defensive abilities
- **Architect**: Support/Tank hybrid with shields and party protection
- **Hacker**: DPS class specializing in high damage and area attacks
- **PenTester**: Stealth DPS with evasion and critical hits
- **Tester**: Support/Analyst with scanning and defensive boosts
- **Support**: Pure healer with party-wide healing and buffs

Each class has unique abilities, stats, and playstyles!

### 2. Understanding Your Stats
- **HP**: Your health points - if this reaches 0, you lose the battle
- **Mana**: Resource for using special abilities
- **Attack**: Base damage you deal
- **Defense**: Reduces incoming damage

## How to Play

### Basic Gameplay Loop

1. **Explore the World**
   - Move between different locations on the world map
   - Each location has unique environmental effects that impact combat
   - Locations include: Firewall Citadel, Void Cache, Bug Bog, Frozen Sector, and more

2. **Accept Quests**
   - Talk to NPCs to receive main story quests and side missions
   - Quests range from combat challenges to puzzle-solving
   - Complete quests to earn experience, gold, and faction reputation

3. **Enter Combat**
   - Random encounters or quest objectives trigger turn-based battles
   - Choose your actions: Attack, Use Ability, Use Item, or Defend

4. **Combat Phase**
   - **Your Turn**: Select from available actions
     - Basic Attack: Deal normal damage
     - Special Abilities: Use class-specific skills (costs mana)
     - Items: Use potions, buffs, or special items
     - Defend: Reduce incoming damage for this turn

   - **Enemy Turn**: Enemies will attack or use their abilities
   - Combat continues until all enemies are defeated or you run out of HP

5. **Level Up and Improve**
   - Gain experience from battles and quests
   - Level up to increase your stats and unlock new abilities
   - Visit faction bases to improve relationships and access special content

## Key Features

### Character Classes & Abilities
Each class has 3 unique abilities with cooldowns:
- **Debugger**: Debug (debuff enemies), Patch (heal), Inspect (reveal info)
- **Architect**: Design (party shield), Rally (defense boost), Unyielding Spirit (survival)
- **Hacker**: Exploit (high damage), DDOS (area damage over time), Rootkit (damage amp)
- **PenTester**: Probe (analysis), Breach (armor ignore), Stealth (evasion boost)
- **Tester**: Scan (vulnerability detection), Verify (defense boost), Penetration Test (precision)
- **Support**: Patch (single heal), Buffer (party stats), System Restore (group heal)

### Status Effects System
Combat features dynamic status effects:
- **Buffs**: Attack boost, defense increase, regeneration
- **Debuffs**: Vulnerability, damage over time, stat reductions
- Effects can stack and have different durations

### World Exploration
- **6 Unique Locations** with environmental modifiers
- **Faction Bases** for reputation building and special quests
- **Environmental Effects** that change combat dynamics

### Quest System
- **Main Story Quests**: Linear campaign with plot progression
- **Side Quests**: Optional missions for extra rewards
- **Faction Quests**: Reputation-based missions with faction-specific rewards

### Equipment & Inventory
- **Weapons, Armor, Accessories** with rarity levels (Common to Legendary)
- **Consumables**: Health potions, mana crystals, temporary buffs
- **Crafting Materials** for creating custom equipment

### Faction System
- **4 Major Factions**: NetGuard, CodeWeavers, DataMiners, SystemRunners
- **Reputation Levels**: Allied, Friendly, Respected, Neutral, Suspicious, Hostile
- **Benefits**: Special quests, trading advantages, faction-specific equipment

## Advanced Gameplay Tips

### Combat Strategy
- **Positioning Matters**: Some abilities work better against certain enemy types
- **Resource Management**: Balance mana usage with ability cooldowns
- **Status Effect Synergy**: Combine buffs and debuffs for maximum effectiveness
- **Class Counterplay**: Each class has strengths and weaknesses against others

### Progression Tips
- **Complete Side Quests**: Extra experience and rare equipment
- **Build Faction Reputation**: Unlock exclusive content and allies
- **Experiment with Classes**: Try different playstyles for varied experiences
- **Manage Your Inventory**: Sell unwanted items, keep useful consumables

### Winning Strategies
- **Know Your Class**: Master your character's abilities and optimal situations
- **Environmental Awareness**: Use location bonuses to your advantage
- **Quest Planning**: Choose quests that match your playstyle and goals
- **Equipment Optimization**: Upgrade gear regularly for better performance
